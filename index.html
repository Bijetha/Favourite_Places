Favourite Places

Golden Temple

The Golden Temple holds deep religious significance for Sikhs. It serves as a place of worship where people from all walks of life and different religions are welcome. The central text of Sikhism, the Guru Granth Sahib, is continuously recited inside the temple.

Mysore Palace

The Mysore Palace, also known as the Amba Vilas Palace, is a stunning historical palace located in the city of Mysore in the southern state of Karnataka, India. It is one of the most magnificent royal residences in the country and is renowned for its architectural grandeur, intricate craftsmanship, and rich history.

Taj Mahal

The Taj Mahal is a magnificent ivory-white marble mausoleum situated in Agra, India, on the southern bank of the Yamuna River. It was commissioned in 1632 by the Mughal emperor Shah Jahan to house the tomb of his beloved wife, Mumtaz Mahal, who died during childbirth.
